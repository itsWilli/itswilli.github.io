    Rigging / Blending Menu - April 2021
    So for every modlue for College we had to do a 'scripting' part to coincide with the rest of the module. Since I had prior knowledge, learning MEL scripting/Phyton was pretty easy to do! I created a menu for each module. However, with Rigging I really took it another step with creating 2 seperate menus.
    One was something alluded to by the tutor with creating a way to streamline the 'Colourblending' process as it was extremely tedious to set up. I took it too far and created a full menu for it with the intention for other classmates to use (which they did) and even the option for it to generate a slider already setup with limits and everything to just work!
    The other is 'Rigging Menu' full of useful shortcuts to help speed up the workflow of creating rigs inside of Maya. I made sure everything was concise and made sense. I even made custom icons! I was given the highest marks for that module and the tutor asked me to create a help sheet for future people to help with understanding programming!
